Here's why you might want to quit carbs

September 29, 2021 by Jason DeCambre

Carbohydrates have long been proposed to be what should make up the majority of our diets. But did you know that the traditional food pyramid was NOT made by the US Departnment of Health? That's right -- it was made by the US Department of AGRICULTURE. Let that sink in. The USDA has long advocated for subsidies for cheap foodstuffs, which tend to be carbohydrates. This is supposed to help many disadvantaged persons and farmers. But why might this be a problem?

The human body uses sugars (a form of carbohydrate) for energy. Without enough sugars, your body starts to burn fat for energy. Ever wanted to lose weight? Now you know to cut down on carbs. Also, 90% of your serotonin is produced by your gut bacteria -- and if the bacteria that feeds on carbs out-competes the serotonin-producing bacteria then you get moody and irritable all the time. Not to mention your blood sugar fluctuates way more on a high-carb diet. So maybe you'd want to try going without carbs for a month. You could just attain your dream body!

COVID and authoritarianism

September 29, 2021 by Jason DeCambre

Have you ever wondered why many persons around the world seem so willing to give up their freedoms to comply with lockdowns that are allegedly to slow the spread of the COVID-19 virus? Have you wondered why there are also so many "anti-vaxxers", "anti-maskers" or other persons who seem to be on the other side of the spectrum? The parasite stress hypothesis (PSH) offers some insight to that.

The PSH proposes that the greater a locale's vulnerability to infectious diseases, the greater the prevalence of authoritarian attitudes on the individual level and therefore the greater likelihood of the prevailing government to be authoritarian. The correlation between infection vulnerability and authoritarianism is greater than that between IQ and grades! So why the dissenting sides? Some political leaders support "containment measures", and others are against them. Many follow either of the 2 authorities without considering the research for themselves or being wary of misinformation, which reflects an individual authoritarian attitude in any case. So let's keep ourselves informed and stay rational. Biology is not on our side in this case.
